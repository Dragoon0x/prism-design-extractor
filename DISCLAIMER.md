## Do Your Own Research (DYOR)

Prism uses large language models (Claude vision + reasoning) plus deterministic heuristics to extract design systems from URLs, screenshots, and PDFs. **AI-generated outputs can be wrong.** Palettes may miss colors or pick up noise. Typography detection may substitute families. Component bounding boxes may be off. Semantic role assignments are best-effort guesses. Audit findings are suggestions, not verdicts.

**Always verify Prism's outputs before using them in production.** Treat every generated token, component scaffold, contrast audit, and consistency suggestion as a PR for human review — not as ground truth.
